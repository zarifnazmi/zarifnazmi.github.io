Travify

Travify is a Final Year Project developed to assist for people who wants to travel in groups by providing them facility and structured communication that supports collaboration between travel members during the planning, during the travel and post travel.

The application is built using React Native with Express Node js & Firebase. Code is maintained at Github. This mobile app has been successfully deployed to Google Play in production mode.

View on Google Play.
